# Demo PDF source

`NIST.SP.800-63-4-excerpt.pdf` is the first 6 pages of:

> **NIST Special Publication 800-63-4 — Digital Identity Guidelines** (July 2025)
> National Institute of Standards and Technology, U.S. Department of Commerce
> <https://doi.org/10.6028/NIST.SP.800-63-4>

It is a U.S. Government work and **not subject to copyright in the United States**
(17 U.S.C. § 105); NIST requests attribution, given above. Included here solely to
demonstrate rmdv's PDF→Markdown viewing on a real, published document. Trimmed to
6 pages with `qpdf` to keep the repo small.
