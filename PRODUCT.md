# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers and writers working with Markdown workspaces. They need to move quickly
between files, inspect document structure, and navigate mindmaps primarily from the
keyboard.

## Product Purpose

`mdv` is a focused Markdown workspace viewer and editor. It makes files, previews,
document structure, and workspace navigation immediately accessible while keeping
the interaction model lightweight and efficient.

## Brand Personality

Fast, efficient, minimalistic. The interface should feel direct and calm, with each
visible element earning its place in the workflow.

## Anti-references

Avoid unnecessary decoration, ornamental surfaces, distracting motion, and UI that
competes with the document or mindmap being navigated.

## Design Principles

- Make common keyboard actions obvious and close to the active workspace.
- Keep navigation responsive and preserve the user’s chosen panel state.
- Prefer concise, familiar controls over explanatory chrome.
- Let content and structure remain the visual focus.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No additional requirements were specified beyond preserving the existing keyboard-
first interaction model. Keep controls operable without relying on color alone and
avoid introducing motion that is necessary to understand or complete an action.
